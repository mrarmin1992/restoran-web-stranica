About us

Welcome to Come a Casa, your number one source for all food. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of food, with an emphasis on quality, taste and your wish.

Founded in 2000 by Luigi Giovano, Come a Casa has come a long way from its beginnings in Toscana. When Luigi first started out, his passion for cooking, drove them to start their own business.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
